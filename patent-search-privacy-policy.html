Privacy Policy for Patent Boolean Search Generator

Last updated: November 29, 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Email addresses for account creation and management
  • Authentication credentials for login purposes
  • Payment information through Stripe's secure processing
  • Search history and generated queries
  • Custom synonym preferences

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Managing your account and subscription
  • Processing payments through Stripe
  • Saving and retrieving your search history
  • Customizing your search experience

3. Data Storage

  • User authentication data is stored securely in Firebase
  • Payment information is processed and stored by Stripe
  • Search history and preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • Custom synonyms are stored in Firebase Firestore

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase for authentication and data storage
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • RapidAPI Words API for synonym suggestions

All third-party services adhere to strict security standards and their own privacy policies.

5. Data Protection

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • We never sell your personal information
  • We do not share your data with unauthorized third parties
  • Payment information is handled directly by Stripe's secure platform

6. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or via email.
